Shipping Bags

With 100 billion deliveries, online orders produce xx tonnes of trash every year. And this amount is growing and growing. One of our goals is to reduce waste while producing our products. Since our business model is based on webshop orders, we feel responsible to also take care that our products are arriving at your doorstep in the most sustainable way possible. Since even recycled cardboard boxes aren't sustainable enough - because they are adding to the singe used trash as well - we are also producing our own shipping bags. Like our COVERs they are made from recyled ressources only and last a lifetime. When this day has arrived they can be recycled again and again.

[ RE ] USE ME!

More than 1 bllion trees are being cut down each year to produce cardboard packaging for e-commerce.

What to do with the Shipping Bag after delivery?

You can either keep the shipping bag and use it for your next shipment or you can send it back to us with some of the old clothes you would have otherwise thrown away. We will make sure that they will reincarnate as a new COVER and will live on forever in this reycling process.

In case you should decide to return your order, please re-use the shipping bag for this purpose.
