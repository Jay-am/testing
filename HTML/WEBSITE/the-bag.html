Shipping Bags

With 103 billion deliveries, online orders produce xx tonnes of trash every year. And this amount is growing and growing. But it is getting worse! More than 3 billion trees are being cut down each year to produce 241 million tonnes of cardboard packaging for e-commerce. And this is just data from 2019! During Covid online orders skyrocketed. In the second quarter of 2020 digital sales ballooned 71 percent and 55 percent in the third,creating a wave of packages and packaging that is ultimately destined for the landfill. Since our business model is based on online orders, we feel responsible to also take care that our products are arriving at your doorstep in the most sustainable way possible.

THE BAG

Since even recycled cardboard boxes aren't sustainable – because they are adding to the single-use trash – we are producing our own shipping bags.

Like our COVERS, THE BAGS are made from recyled ressources only and last a lifetime. When this day has arrived they as well can be recycled again and again.

What to do with the Shipping Bag after delivery?

You can either keep the shipping bag and use it for your next shipment or you can send it back to us with some of the old clothes you would have otherwise thrown away. We will make sure that they will reincarnate as a new COVER and will live on forever in this reycling process.

The same goes for the shipping bag. We will use it for the next delivery to make someone else happy.



NOTE

In case you should decide to return your order, please reuse the shipping bag for this purpose.
