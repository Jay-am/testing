HOW TO BECOME A RETAILER

STEP 1: SEND YOUR INQUIRY

If you are a concept store, gift shop or any other store, we would love to get to know you! Please send a message to wholesale@cover.international. We'd appreciate it, if you would shortly introduce yourself, describe what you do and share your vision of our future partnership.

STEP 2: GETTING TO KNOW EACH OTHER (INTEREXCHANGE)

We bet you have a lot of questions you would love to ask us as well. We will take all the time needed to really get to know each other so we can build a meaningful relationship. Weather on the phone, via video call or in person. We will discuss which amounts are perfect for the size of your store and finalize the details.

STEP 3: GETTING SERIOUS (ORDER + SHIPPING)

Are you ready for our common future? Now it’s time to commit. After you placed the order we will make sure that this is the beginning of a wonderful long lasting relationship.

STEP 4: YOUR ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT

As usual we will plant a tree for every purchase made to recover forests, even when this purchase is initiated by you. The more COVERs you are selling the more you’re helping to recover our planet. You can communicate this to your customers and we will make sure to put your company on our website and promote you as a trusted partner.
