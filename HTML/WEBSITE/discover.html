Get inspired

Our COVERS themselves are inspired by a Japanese (Furoshiki) and Korean (Bojagi) tradition of using fabrics to wrap items. We love this eco-friendly alternative to wrapping paper so much, that we we're looking for ways to make them even more sustainable and widespread. Using ony recycled materials while producing items with a western touch, we're trying to make living zero-waste as convinient as possible.

How to use your COVER

Unlike wrapping paper, fabric gift wraps are flexible and will adapt to any shape. This is why wrapping with a fabric wrap is extremely easy. Your presents will look stylish and pretty in just seconds and with the huge range of wrapping techniques you can up your style every time.
