Returns

We gladly accept returns - on both new arrivals and sale items - and offer you 14 days of return from the day you receive your package. If you wish to return your item(s), please follow the guideline below. Please note that returns are at your own expense.

How to return items

1

GET IN CONTACT (SAY HI)

Please send a message to returns@cover.international with your name and order number stating what you want to return.

2

RETURN YOUR ORDER (SAY BYE)

Please return your package to:

COVER GmbH
Logistic Center
Düsseldorfer Str. 6-8
68219 Mannheim
Germany

Please note that all items must be returned unused and in original packaging.

3

GET A REFUND (COMPENSATION)

Once we have received and accepted the returned item(s), you will receive a refund. Depending on the payment method and issuer, it may take a couple of hours up to a few days until the refund is credited to your account.

Please note that from the day we have processed your return, it usually takes 1 to 3 business days before the amount is available on your account.

Please note

All orders are sent in sustainable shipping bags made from recycled materials. If you choose to return your order, please re-use the packaging.

The responsibility for the item(s) is yours until the item(s) has been delivered on the listed return address. For your own safety, please keep your postage receipt with tracking number until you have received your refund.

At the moment, we do not offer exchanges. If you need a different size or color, please place a new order for the right one.
