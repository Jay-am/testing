Sustainability

Sustain­ability has been the reason why we started COVER and it will always be the most integral part of our company. We are constantly striving to further minimize our environ­mental foot­print because we know that sus­tain­abil­ity is an ongoing process and that there is always room for improve­ment. At COVER we believe in our respon­si­bil­i­ty as a company - for the environ­ment and society. We aim for corporate trans­parency, close relation­ships with our partners as well as our community.

UN­DER­STAND­ING THE EN­VI­RON­MEN­TAL IMPACTS

Min­imis­ing the en­vi­ron­men­tal foot­print involves under­stand­ing the en­vi­ron­men­tal impacts.
This is why we took a close look at every stage in the process.

MATERIALS

We are not touching new ressources. All our products are made of recycled (existing) organic materials. After it's life cycle, every single product can be recycled. This way no materials and resources are wasted and ending up as litter or in oceans.

PRODUCTION

WORK FORCE

We believe that world-class manufacturing begins with the respect for people and our planet. Because sus­tain­abil­ity is just as much about social standards as environ­mental issues, we are choosing suppliers who share our values and commitment. Working with factories that share the same values gives us the confidence that our production standards are fully met. We work with suppliers that respect and uphold the rights of employees and workers throughout the supply chain by ensuring they are treated fairly, provided with safe, healthy and respectful workplaces and guaranteed a minimum wage.

ENERGY

All the energy fueling the manufacturing and distribution of the products is from renewable energy to minimize our footprint even more.

PACKAGING

We are striving for zero waste. We believe that every little detail counts and makes a big difference in the long term. Since our eco friendly gift wrappings are used to wrap other products, we decided against a separate packaging for them. This way we can make sure that no tree will die just to present our products more nicely.

DELIVERY

All webshop orders are send in sustainable shipping bags made from recycled materials and shipped environ­mentally friendly with DHL GoGreen. Within the parcel, we only include necessities like a packing slip (printed on recycled grass paper, to make sure that no tree was killed and the CO2 usage is as low as possible), whereas other documents are provided digitally to minimize paper usage. Additionally, our customers can make use of our paperless return system.

REVERSE LOGISTICS

PRODUCT PRESENTATION

Good product images are one of the most important elements to avoid disappointment and returns and thus a lot of resources that are needed to deliver and return the products. Hence, we include high-resolution product shots with multiple angles. We also provide videos on how to use our products and display pictures on how to style your gifts for more inspiration.

SIZE GUIDE

In hope to reduce orders with multiple sizes and returns based on wrong sizes (which will also result in extra packaging and shipping pollution) we provide sizing examples as well as a size guide.

CARE INSTRUCTIONS

Our gift wrappings meant to last a lifetime. In order for them to do so, it is important to be mindful of how to take care of them. Therfore we provide care instructions to extend the life cycle of our COVERs.

DISPOSAL

Last but not least, we are recycling damaged products. If your COVER has lost it's shine you can send it back to us and we will take care of the disposal - for the material to be recycled again and again to stretch out it’s use beyond it's lifetime as a gift wrap even further.
