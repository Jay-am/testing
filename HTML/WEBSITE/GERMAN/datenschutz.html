Privacy Policy

It is very important for us to ensure the protection of your personal information when you use our services. That’s why your privacy connected to the use of our website is processed and stored with utmost care. The following section sets out to explain how we process and use the personal data you leave and/or provide when you visit our website and use our services.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

1. THE COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

You will always be informed in connection with the collection of your personal data – either by submitting the personal data yourself or by accepting our cookies. The personal data may include your name, e‑mail address, similar identification data, information about an online purchase and your navigation on the website.

When submitting your data on our website, it will always be stated whether the submission is voluntary or necessary for completing the desired action, such as completing a transaction at our webshop.

Your personal data is collected when:

  • You make a purchase on the webshop
  • You sign up for our newsletter
  • You create a client account
  • You participate in a competition
  • You actively engage with the website in another way

If you use your e‑mail address in more than one of the above cases, your personal data will only be collected and registered in one place. This way your information is already stored when you shop with us, and you will not receive the same marketing material more than once.

USE

2. THE USE OF PERSONAL DATA

The personal data we collect is used in connection with:

  • Completion of orders on our webshop
  • Sign-ups for our newsletter
  • Management of client accounts
  • Management of our customer club
  • Participation in competitions and other events
  • Send-outs of other marketing material, including invitations to events and discount codes
  • Collection and evaluation of consumer reviews
  • Overview of purchase history
  • Statistics
  • Other marketing initiatives

Please note that we will only use your personal data to send marketing material if you have given your consent. We keep statistics about our users’ use of our website and which products our users prefer in order to improve the website. This data does not contain personal data. We do not sell or disclose information about how our users navigate and use our website, neither information about your purchase history to third parties.

TRANSFERRING

3. TRANSFERRING DATA TO OTHER CONTROLLERS

In general, we do not transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent. However, information used for delivery of goods will be transferred to the shipping company we use.

Under specific circumstances and with reference to legislation, it might be necessary to transfer information to public authorities or the police. For example, information may be disclosed to the police in case of suspicion of credit card fraud.

In the case of a re-organization, full or partial sale of the company, any disclosure in such connection will be in accordance with current legislation for the processing of personal data.

DISCLOSING

4. DISCLOSING DATA TO DATA PROCESSORS

Your personal data is disclosed to our partners who deliver services on behalf of us, for example in relation to sending out newsletters. These partners only process the personal data on behalf of us and in accordance with our high security standards.

TRANSFERRING

5. TRANSFERRING DATA TO THIRD COUNTRIES

As we use the services of the Canadian platform Shopify when you shop at our website, your personal data is transferred to Canada. As a Canadian organization, Shopify handles your personal data according to the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), which means they handle your personal data according to the highest security standards. We also use the services of MailChimp, located in the USA. As an American organization, MailChimp handles your personal data according to the EU-U.S Privacy Shield Framework, which means they also handle your data according to the highest international standards. The Commission has decided that the level of protection is sufficient in both countries. You can read more about secure third countries here.

DELETING

6. DELETING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We will delete your personal data when we no longer need to process them in relation to one or more of the purposes set out in section 4. However, the data may be processed and stored for a longer period in anonymized form.

USE

7. THE USE OF COOKIES

We use cookies on our website. You can read more about the use of cookies in our Cookie Policy.

SECURITY

8. YOUR SECURITY

We strive to protect your information the best way possible, which is why we have implemented security measures to ensure that our internal procedures meet our high security policy standards – for instance by using Secure Socket Layers (SSL), HTTPS and PSD2. Accordingly, we strive to protect the quality and integrity of your personal data.

RIGHTS

9. YOUR RIGHTS

You are at any time entitled to be informed of the personal data about you that we process, but with certain legislative exceptions. You also have the right to object to the collection and further processing of your personal data including profiling/automated decision-making. Furthermore, you have the right to have your personal data rectified, erased or blocked. Moreover, you have the right to receive information about you that you have provided to us, and the right to have this information transmitted to another data controller (data portability).

WITHDRAWAL

10. WITHDRAWAL OF CONSENT

You may, at any time, withdraw any consent you have given and we will delete your personal data, unless we can continue the processing based on another purpose. If you wish to withdraw your consent, please contact us at hello@cover.earth.

LINKS

11. LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

Our website may contain links to other websites or to integrated sites. We are not responsible for the content of the websites of other companies or for the practices of such companies regarding the collection of personal data. When you visit other websites, you should read the owners’ policies on the protection of personal data and other relevant policies.

AMENDMENT

12. THE AMENDMENT OF DATA

If you want us to update, amend or delete the personal data that we have recorded about you, wish to get access to the data being processed about you, or if you have any questions concerning the above guidelines, please contact us at hello@cover.earth.

COMPLAINTS

13. COMPLAINTS

If you wish to appeal against the processing of your personal data, please contact us by e‑mail as indicated above.
