Delivery

All webshop orders are send in our sus­tain­able shipping bags made from recycled materials and shipped en­vi­ron­men­tal­ly friendly with DHL GoGreen. If you choose to return your order, please re-use this packaging.

THE DELIVERY PROCESS

1

ORDER CONFIRMATION

After placing your order you will receive an order confirmation with all details of your purchase.
All orders placed before 12pm (CET) on a working day are usually shipped within the same day. In busy saleperiods, we do our very best to dispatch your order within 1 to 2 working days.

2

SHIPPING CONFIRMATION

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an e-mail confirmation. On the same day, you will receive a tracking number by e-mail sent directly from the courier.

Please note

DELIVERING CHARGES

The prices in our online store do not include delivery charges.

The 19% German Sales Tax/VAT is not added to orders sent to a destination outside the EU, but these orders may be subject to local taxes and/or import duties at your own expense. All fees regarding local customs, including handling, duties and taxes, will be sent to you directly from the courier.

DELAYS

We cannot be held liable for any delays caused by the courier or local customs.
