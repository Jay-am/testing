Delivery

All webshop orders are send in sustainable shipping bags made from recycled materials and shipped environmentally friendly with DHL GoGreen. If you choose to return your order, please re-use the packaging. If not, please dispose of the packaging thoughtfully in appropriate recycling bins in order for the material to be recycled again and again.

THE DELIVERY PROCESS

STEP 1: ORDER CONFIRMATION

After placing your order you will receive an order confirmation email with all details of your purchase.

All orders placed before 12.00 am (CET) on a working day are usually shipped within the same day. In busy sale periods, we do our very best to dispatch your order within 1 to 2 working days from the day you place your order.

STEP 2: SHIPPING CONFIRMATION

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an e-mail confirmation. On the same day, you will receive a tracking number by e-mail sent directly from the courier.
