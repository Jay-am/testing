Delivery

We are shipping green and offer free shipping on all orders within Germany above 50 Euro and free international shipping on all orders above 100 Euro.

All orders placed before 12.00 am (CET) on a working day are usually shipped within the same day. In busy sale periods, we do our very best to dispatch your order within 1 to 2 working days from the day you place your order.
