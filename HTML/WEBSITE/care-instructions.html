Care

At COVER we create beautiful eco friendly gift wrappings meant to last a lifetime. In order for them to do so, it is important to be mindful of how to take care of them. This will vary from piece to piece (dependent on the fabrics that have been used), which is why it is essential to always read the care label carefully before washing. That being said, we have collected some general guidelines for you to adhere to, to extend the life cycle of your COVER.

Wash less, wash right, pass it on and recycle.

1

WASHING

Wash (only if necessary) on low temperatures (lukewarm or cold water) in a gentle cycle, low spin washing programme.

2

DRYING

Air dry instead of putting it in the dryer – not only is this less rough on the fabric, it also uses less energy.

2

DETERGENT

Choose a mild environmentally friendly detergent to protect the integrity of the fibers.

2

MACHINE LOAD

Try not to overcrowd the washing machine with too many items at once as this can cause the fabric to lose its shape.

2

RECYCLE

When the COVER has lost its shine, recycle it … or send it back to us so we can take care of it. By recycling your COVER you can reduce the impact on the environment even further.

PRO TIP: If you really want to take good care of your COVER protect it in a laundry bag when washing in the machine.
