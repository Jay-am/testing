Getting Personal

Are you looking for a creative and sustainable way to wrap your presents while also standing out as an environmentally-conscious organization that is giving back to customers, business partners, nature and mankind? Or would you like to use eco-friendly custom gift wrappings for your wedding, or other events?

CORPORATE GIFTS
CUSTOMISATION

&

Then let us celebrate the ‘Art of Giving’. while not only making the presentee happy but also make the world and future generations a present of reducing, reusing and recycling waste and also recovering forests and thus working against climate change.

Our eco-friendly custom gift wrappings are perfect for corporate gifts, in-store gift packages, wedding and shower favors, or promotional giveaways.

We want to help you to wrap your products in ways that adhere to your values while communicating your story, building your brand and developing a loyal customer base.

And the best thing?

The reusable nature of the product provides a powerful advertising opportunity for your brand. Instead of spending money on paper gift wrapping that goes directly into a landfill, and being responsible for cutting down trees, you can now send your customers off with something that will be passed on again and again, putting even more eyes on your brand.

Please note

The minimum order quantity for custom made COVERs is 100 pieces. You can roughly estimate a lead time of 30 to 60 days. This depends on how quickly we’re getting through the design process and where the order has to get shipped to.

HOW IT WORKS

1

SEND YOUR INQUIRY

Send us an inquiry to custom@cover.international with your desired quantity and personalization preferences (logo, colors, design). If you already have a design in mind feel free to include it in your email.

2

THE DESIGN PROCESS

Based on your preferences, we will create a custom preview of your customized COVER so you can see how the final product will look like. We are also happy to work with your agency to create your custom artwork. All we want is to create something that is unique for your business and communicates what matters to you.

3

FINALIZING THE DETAILS

Once you are satisfied with the design, we’ll send you a quote for approval and all the details to get the production started.

4

GETTING SERIOUS
(PRODUCTION + SHIPPING)

After your approval we will launch the production process. As soon as the COVERs are ready to start their journey, we will send you a notification with all the details to track your order.

5

YOUR ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT CERTIFICATE

As usual we will plant trees for your purchase to recover forests. And as always they will get your name. As soon as these trees are planted you will receive a certificate with their location as well as a picture and the environmental impact these trees and purchased COVERs will have on our planet.
